About Us

Our practice started as a family business as our Dr. Smith inherited the practice from his father who inherited it from his grandfather. We keep their goals and culture from before alive as we continue to practice as a family. We treat all of our own staff like family.
Our purpose is to provide affordable, but effective treatment to our patients. We follow preventative measures and want to stop and prevent further issues.
Our goals are to remain affordable to the general public. We will strive to accept all forms of health insurance and prevent patients from being overcharged

Services

We provide a large range of services including a simple cleaning or deep cleaning of the the teeth and gums, invisalign installation, along with different cosmetic options and oral surgeries.
